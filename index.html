How it works

Lightweight AI, powerful results — all running on your iPhone.

The process

From sound to screen in real time

1

Microphone picks up speech

The app listens to the conversation around you using your iPhone's microphone.

2

On-device AI processes audio

A lightweight AI model transcribes speech and identifies each unique speaker — all locally, no internet needed.

3

Camera matches voice to face

The front or rear camera pairs each speaker's voice to their face using computer vision.

4

Words appear on screen

Each word is displayed with the speaker's name and face so you always know who said what.

Technology

Built with accessibility in mind

Speech processing

Custom lightweight speech-to-text model optimized for real-time use on mobile hardware.

Speaker diarization

Identifies and separates multiple speakers in a single audio stream.

Face linking

Computer vision ties each speaker ID to a face in the camera view.

About Audivize

A student-built project with a real-world mission.

Our mission

Empowering 430 million people to join the conversation

Audivize was born from a simple question: why should your ability to communicate depend on others learning sign language? We're building on-device AI that makes group conversations accessible to everyone with hearing loss — no special hardware, no privacy tradeoffs, no barriers.
